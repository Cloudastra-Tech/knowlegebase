Cloudastra Academy — Public Handbook

About us:
Cloudastra Academy was founded in 2021 and is based in India.
We train small businesses and students to use AI tools without needing a data science team.
The academy employs teachers and senior staff to run its programs.

Refund policy:
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
After 30 days, refunds are given as account credit only.

Support hours:
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm IST.
We do not offer phone support on weekends, but email is answered within 24 hours.

Course pricing:
The Starter plan costs 500 rupees per month and includes 1000 AI queries.
The Pro plan costs 2000 rupees per month and includes unlimited AI queries.

Student stipend (public information):
Students enrolled in the AI Fellowship receive a monthly stipend of 3000 rupees.
The stipend is paid on the 1st of every month for the duration of the 6-month program.
Students must complete weekly assignments to remain eligible for the stipend.
